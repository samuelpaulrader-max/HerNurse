About HerNurse

Every woman deserves a nurse who has the time to truly listen.

We educate and empower women to make confident decisions about their health.

Our story

HerNurse began with a frustration shared by women everywhere: feeling rushed, unheard, and left with more questions than answers. We knew nurses — among the most trusted professionals in healthcare — could offer something different.

So we built a service centered entirely on women's health, where experienced registered nurses have the time to listen, educate, and support. Whether you're navigating a new life stage or simply want a knowledgeable guide in your corner, HerNurse is here.
